System Overview

The system uses dermatoglyphic fingerprint patterns (Arch, Loop, Whorl), ridge count, and ridge density — extracted via OpenCV — as biometric features. These are fused with physiological health parameters (Age, BMI, Family History of Diabetes) and classified using a Random Forest Classifier trained on 3,000 records.

FAQ

How accurate is the prediction?

The model achieves 97.8% accuracy on the test set. However, this is a research prototype and not a clinical tool.

What data is required?

A fingerprint image (.bmp/.png/.jpg), your Age, BMI, and Family History of Diabetes — no blood test needed.

Can I use this for diagnosis?

No. This is a screening research tool only. Always consult a qualified medical professional for diagnosis.

Is my data secure?

This is a local research prototype. Data is not stored permanently and resets on server restart.
